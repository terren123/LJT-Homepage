**First Author**

This paper discovers a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models (LLMs) and explores its implications for understanding and improving LLM truthfulness and interpretability. The code is available in the Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane GitHub repository.

**Co-authors**: Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He
